Necks of Dresses

There are two main categories that dress necks fall under: Necklines and Collars.

Halter

Wraps Around Neck, Usually With A Low-Cut Back

Illusion

Transparent Fabric Fitted At Neckline Extending To Bustline

Plunging

Low Cut Extending To Breasts Or Waist

Scoop

Low And Rounded With A Shallow “U” Shape

Mandarin

High Band Collar With Gap At Center Front
